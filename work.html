    • What exactly is UX?

    UX stands for User Experience. That means I specialize in professional empathy and I get to make design feel more human. My job goes beyond just making things look pretty. In fact here are my favourite things I regularly do with UX design:

    • Design wonderful and engaging interactive experiences for consumers.
    • Tailor web and mobile applications to both the needs of developers and customers.
    • Find the sweet spot between what the company is trying to say and what the consumer is supposed to understand.
